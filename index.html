VISION AND MISSION

VISION

To be a center of excellence in Computer Science and Engineering education, research, and innovation, producing competent professionals who contribute to the advancement of technology and society.

MISSION

  • To provide high-quality education in Computer Science and Engineering, fostering critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and ethical values.
  • To promote research and innovation in emerging areas of Computer Science and Engineering, encouraging students and faculty to contribute to technological advancements.
  • To establish strong industry collaborations, enabling students to gain practical experience and stay updated with the latest trends in the field.
  • To create a supportive learning environment that nurtures creativity, teamwork, and lifelong learning among students.
